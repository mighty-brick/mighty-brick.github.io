Rules

1. Games must incorporate the sub-theme in some way.

2. All work on the game must be done within the duration of the contest, this includes code, art, world-design, etc.

3. Outside assets can be used but must be disclosed and the number of assets used will affect your score.

4. Games can be singleplayer or multiplayer, and optionally VR. However games cannot be VR exclusive.

5. Multiple developers on a game are allowed, so team up with some buds.

6. The deadline is strict and participants are not allowed to push edits to their game after that time.

7. Games can be uploaded, tweaked & played before the deadline. (this may help in finding any major bugs before the contest ends.)

Judging Criteria


Gameplay & Replayability

We will be playing each submission thoroughly, so aiming to make a replayable, fun & bug-free experience is important.


Use of sub-theme & originality

Try your best to incorporate the sub-theme in your own unique way.


Artwork & Polish

Good looking consistent styles will score more points over a mix-mash of visually colliding assets.
